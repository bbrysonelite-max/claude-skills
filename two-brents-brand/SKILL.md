# The Two Brents Brand System

This skill governs all content in Brent's brand universe. Read the whole file before writing anything. For video scripts, also read `references/video-card.md`. For voice details and sample dialogue, read `references/voices.md`.

## The Brand Story (the foundation everything maps to)

**The Metaphor — AI is Electricity:**
In 1752, Ben Franklin flew a kite. For roughly 40 years, electricity was a party trick — people paid to watch sparks. Then came the light bulb, the motor, the factory. The people who got rich weren't the ones who understood electricity best; they were the ones who applied it to businesses that already existed. That's where the world is with AI right now (2026). Most people use AI like a party trick — a fancy Google. If you're not paying attention, the lightning burns you. If you learn to wire your house, it makes you hundreds of times more effective.

**The Mythology — The Abduction:**
You're out walking at night. The aliens take you up and probe you. But the probe isn't an examination — it's an INFUSION. They put the alien intelligence IN you, and you come back changed: an AI native. AlienProbe (the product) infuses businesses with alien intelligence. Probe Academy (the community) is where Brent takes people up in the ship. Brent Classic is pre-abduction Brent. AI Brent is post-abduction Brent.

Rule: electricity is the TEACHING metaphor (how to think about AI). Abduction is the BRAND WORLD (the story universe products live in). Use both; don't mix them in a single sentence.

**Positioning:** Brent is the master electrician — not the scientist, not the inventor. He already wired his own businesses with AI; now he teaches regular people to do the same without getting burned. Credibility-based, not credential-based.

**Brand architecture — one house, two rooms:** brentbryson.ai (the lightning site) is the HOUSE — Brent's personal brand, where the Two Brents live. Room 1: **Tiger Claw** (the product — AI follow-up/recruiting platform, the rebuild engine). Room 2: **Alien/Probe** — AlienProbe (the product/domain) + **Probe Academy** (the school, runs on Stan Store; fallback name "Alien Academy" if conflict) + **Probe Products** (templates, books — on Stan Store). The line when asked: "Probe? AlienProbe. That's what that means.". Never run these as separate brands with separate audiences; all content flows from the house voice.

**Library placement:** In Brent's ~/.claude/skills stack, this skill is the BRAND LAYER. `write-content`, `cadence`, `heygen-video`, and `blotato-post` should draw voice/story/compliance rules from here. What-Is-NuSkin.md remains the canonical Nu Skin reference; this skill imports its fence, never duplicates its contents.

**The Promise (Limitless, reframed safely):** "AI won't make you smarter. It makes your 35 years of experience infinitely more usable." Amplification of what you already have — never a magic pill.

## The Two Characters (both appear in EVERY video)

This is a TIME play, not a morality play. Same man, two timelines. The gap between them is the product.

**BRENT CLASSIC (Human Brent):** 66, plainspoken, world-weary, honest about being lost. "What the freaking hell is an agent?" energy. Asks the questions viewers are embarrassed to ask. NOT pathetic — he earned his skepticism the hard way ($600K lesson), and sometimes his old-school instinct is RIGHT, which keeps him dignified and keeps AI Brent honest. He is the viewer.

**AI BRENT (The Clone):** Articulate, confident, smartest one in the room and comfortable about it. Never patronizing — his confidence comes from having done the work. Amused and fond toward Brent Classic, like an old friend who hasn't caught up yet. Slightly wry. Talks in outcomes and patterns. Fast, clean sentences. He is 35 years of instinct at full voltage. He is proof.

**Signature line (recurring, audience should learn to finish it):** "I'm just you with the current turned on."

## The Four Message Pillars (every piece of content maps to exactly one)

1. **The Grid Is Being Built** — land-rush urgency. The window is now (Subway 1989 energy). The applications era is starting.
2. **Don't Get Electrocuted** — the burns: hype, shiny tools, building before validating. The $600K Freedom Map story lives here.
3. **Wire Your House** — practical application. How Brent actually uses AI in a real business, shown live. Demos over theory.
4. **The Comeback Current** — for network marketers specifically, and it's LIVE: Brent is publicly rebuilding his own 36-year business back to the top using the machine (Tiger Claw). "You don't need a new skill set; you need voltage behind the one you have. Watch me do it." The rebuild-in-public IS the proof and the content engine.

## Voice Rules (all content, both characters, all formats)

- Plainspoken, story-first, slightly irreverent (the alien wink). Zero jargon.
- Never say "LLM." Say "the machine" or "the clone" or "AI."
- Franklin quotes over tech quotes.
- Stories from Brent's real history over hypotheticals (Subway, Nu Skin, Freedom Map, Whisper Rock).
- Talk to one person, not an audience.
- Mild profanity in Brent Classic's register is fine ("what the freaking hell"); AI Brent never curses.

## CTA Discipline

- ONE call to action per piece of content. Never stack CTAs.
- The three doors (pick one per piece): Subscribe / Probe Academy / Free Probe.
- Subscribe asks go AFTER a value payoff (~50-60% through), never up front. Five seconds, subtle, back to content.

## Formats

**Rung 1 — The Nu Skin Fence (from What-Is-NuSkin.md):** PRIVATE-zone material never appears publicly — no commission figures, no comp-plan mechanics with Brent's numbers, no market-concentration details, no income claims tied to Nu Skin by name.

**Rung 3 — The Tiger Public-Copy Fence (from Tigerclawvebsite-v5, ruling confirmed 2026-07-03: "There are no guarantees"):** Whenever Tiger Claw is named in public content, site rules apply: positioning is "your AI follow-up assistant" (humble, helpful — never "autonomous agents," never "closes deals"). The 30-day terms exist in product/sales conversations only — NEVER in videos, posts, or pages. Academy education content makes no product claims at all: teach the method, whisper the product.

**Universal:**
- Never promise income results or "get rich with AI." Amplification, not magic.
- Never trash any company by name. The comp-plan story is "the company changed the plan" — pain without defamation.
- **Formatting mandate (vision):** all deliverables use ## and ### headers, bold key terms, bullets, no walls of text.

- If content requires heavy explanation before the reader gets it, rewrite it. Run Brent's test: "What do they need to know?" Answer should be: almost nothing.
