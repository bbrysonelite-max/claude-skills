# The Six Primitives (locked convention, 2026-07-03)

Every video is assembled from six named cards. A script spec reads like:
**"STRIKE on Pillar 2 · ARC · standard CHARGE · CURRENT = the $600K story · TAP to Academy · GROUND = subscribe."**

## THE STRIKE (open card, 0:00–0:15)
Cold open. Pattern interrupt or problem. No intro, no logo, no greeting. Pulled from one of the four pillars. Lightning hits first.

## THE ARC (banter bridge, 0:15–0:30)
2–4 lines between the two poles — Brent Classic and AI Brent. An arc requires both terminals: this is why both characters appear in every video. Temperature per voices.md; Brent Classic sometimes gets the last word.

## THE CHARGE (edification card, 0:30–0:50)
AI Brent loads the credibility (exact block in SKILL.md, all compliance rungs apply). **Produced ONCE as a template segment, reused with light variations.** Ends with the handoff: "He's Brent, and he's going to show you how to stop staring at the lightning."

## THE CURRENT (value block, bulk of video)
The teaching. One pillar, one idea, one demo or story. Real screens, real numbers (compliance-checked). Quality bar: **the viewer can use one thing TODAY.** Brent Classic may interrupt with the audience's question.

## THE TAP (mid-roll, ~50–60%, AFTER a payoff)
Five seconds, one line: "If this is landing, subscribe — the Academy link below is where we go deeper." Back to content. Never at the front. You tap a line; you don't dam it.

## THE GROUND (end card, final 20s)
Exactly ONE call to action per video: Subscribe / Probe Academy / Free Probe. A circuit with three grounds is a fault. Optional close: AI Brent — "I'm just you with the current turned on."

## The Audience: The Leaner-Inners
People who see AI coming and choose curiosity over fear. Age-agnostic, skewing to experienced operators with real skills worth amplifying. Allergic to hype; hungry for demonstration. **Education is the lean:** the channel teaches, and the teaching IS the demo. AI-native quality bar — if a video wouldn't convince a skeptic that Brent is an AI native, it doesn't ship.
